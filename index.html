Our History

From tennis courts to the premier pickleball club in Connecticut.

Harborview was established in 1987 by a group of twelve local families who wanted a place to play tennis close to the shore. For more than three decades, the club served as a quiet institution for Harborview and surrounding communities.

In 2018, responding to growing member interest, the club converted four tennis courts to pickleball and introduced its first structured open-play sessions. The response exceeded all expectations. By 2020, pickleball had become the club's primary sport and twelve of our sixteen courts were dedicated to it.

Today, Harborview Pickleball Club is recognized as one of the top private pickleball clubs in the Northeast — home to competitive leagues, nationally ranked junior players, and a membership that continues to grow year over year.

Our mission remains what it has always been: provide exceptional facilities, structured programming, and a welcoming community for players who love the game.

Court Rules

Standards of Play

01Proper court shoes required on all surfaces. No running shoes or sandals permitted.
02Court time is limited to 60 minutes when other members are waiting to play.
03Reserved courts are held for 10 minutes. Unclaimed courts are released to walk-on players.
